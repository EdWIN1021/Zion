## Collision Presets

### Collision Enabled

- No Collision
- Query Only (No Physics Collision)
    - Used for spatial queries (Raycasts, sweeps, overlaps)
    - No physics simulation
- Physics Only (No Query Collision)
    - Used for physics simulation
    - No queries
    - Physics engine works on the component
- Collision Enabled (Query and Physics)
    - Queries
    - Physics
