The `UPawnSensingComponent` is a useful component that allows pawns (such as characters or enemies) to detect and sense other pawns within their vicinity. This component is commonly used in games to implement AI perception, enabling characters to detect players or other characters nearby.

```cpp
UPawnSensingComponent* PawnSensing;
PawnSensing = CreateDefaultSubobject<UPawnSensingComponent>(TEXT("PawnSensing"));
```
