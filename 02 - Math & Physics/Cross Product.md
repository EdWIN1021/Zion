
- Area of parallelogram
- The result is a new vector perpendicular to both $\vec{A}$  and $\vec{B}$ (Right Hand Rule)

$$
\lvert \vec{A} \times \vec{B} \rvert
=
\lvert \vec{A} \rvert \, \lvert \vec{B} \rvert \sin \theta
$$

$$
\vec{A} \times \vec{B}
=
-
\bigl(
\vec{B} \times \vec{A}
\bigr)
$$

$$
\vec{A} \times \vec{B}
=
\begin{vmatrix}
\hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\
A_x & A_y & A_z \\
B_x & B_y & B_z
\end{vmatrix}
$$

$$
\vec{A} \times \vec{B}
=
\hat{i}(A_y B_z - A_z B_y)
-
\hat{j}(A_x B_z - A_z B_x)
+
\hat{k}(A_x B_y - A_y B_x)
$$
