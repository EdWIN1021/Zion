- `COND_None`: Default value. The property is always replicated.
- `COND_InitialOnly`: The property is replicated only in the initial packet when the actor is first spawned.
- `COND_OwnerOnly`: The property is replicated only to the client that owns the Actor.
- `COND_SkipOwner`: The property is replicated to everyone except the owner.
- `COND_SimulatedOnly`: Replicated only to clients in the Simulated Proxy role.
- `COND_AutonomousOnly`: Replicated only to the autonomous proxy.
- `COND_SimulatedOrPhysics`: Replicated to simulated proxies or Actors that are simulating physics.
- `COND_InitialOrOwner`: Replicated in the initial packet, or to the owner.
- `COND_ReplayOrOwner`: Replicated only to the owner and the replay system.
- `COND_ReplayOnly`: Replicated only to the replay system, not sent to any normal network clients.
- `COND_SimulatedOnlyNoReplay`: Replicated only to simulated proxies and not recorded in replays.
- `COND_SimulatedOrPhysicsNoReplay`: This means the property will be replicated to **Simulated Proxies** or clients where **physics is being simulated, but it will **not** be recorded into the Replay data.
- `COND_SkipReplay`: Replicates normally to clients but is **not** recorded into the Replay/Demo system.
