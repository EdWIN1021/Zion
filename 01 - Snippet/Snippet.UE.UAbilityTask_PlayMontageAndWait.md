# UAbilityTask_PlayMontageAndWait

Base: UAbilityTask (UAbilityTask%201898d590ff388085ad9dcfa0ebe6de87.md) 
System: GAS
Type: Class

In Unreal Engine's **Gameplay Ability System (GAS)**, **`PlayMontageAndWait`** is a commonly used **`AbilityTask`** for playing animation montages (**`AnimMontage`**) within a **`GameplayAbility`** and **waiting for the montage to complete or be interrupted**.
